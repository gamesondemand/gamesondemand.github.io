Games on Demand Policies

These are the rules and responsibilities for participating in Games on Demand.

  • Your Responsibility
  • You Will Be Asked to Leave If...
  • You Are Awesome If...
  • Our Liability

Your Responsibility

  • All the regular Gen Con policies are still in effect.
  • In order to keep Games on Demand a safe and successful event, attendees must assume some additional responsibility.
  • When you play with us, you hereby agree to the stuff below.

You Will be Asked to Leave if We Decide You Are...

  • Harassing anyone - physically, verbally, emotionally or any other way.
  • Behaving in an aggressive, argumentative, racist, sexist, and/or homophobic way.
  • Messing with other people’s stuff.
  • Taking pictures of anyone without permission (we have a staff photographer you may see).

You Are Awesome & Very Welcome if You...

  • Don’t act like a jerk!
  • Share the spotlight and help others have fun!
  • Embrace a game’s theme and tone!
  • Step away from the table if you need to use your phone or eat!
  • Don’t use "it’s what my character would do" as an excuse!
  • Call out cheating, rules lawyering and other fun-killing behavior!
  • Clean up after yourself and help us keep the space tidy!

Our Liability

  • Games on Demand organizers, volunteers, and sponsors will not be held liable for any damage to or loss of personal items, or any personal injury that transpires during or on the premises of this event.
  • The aforementioned parties will not be held liable for punitive, incidental, consequential or special damages whether or not such damages could have been foreseen and/or prevented.
