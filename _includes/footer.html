The 2025 Gen Con Games on Demand event is happening in the Westin Hotel in Grand Ballroom 3 on the second floor, near the convention center skywalk.

Sessions will run from 10am-10pm on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday and from 10am-2pm on Sunday. You should plan to arrive 10 minutes before the session start time.

While some games might be suitable for players of age 10+, most games this year are for 18+.
